**🌍 Project Pitch: Nairobi Waste Tracker 🚮**

---

## **Slide 1: 🌟 Why Nairobi Needs This**
🚮 Ever wondered why Nairobi’s garbage problem persists? Our city faces waste management issues daily. 
✅ **Introducing Nairobi Waste Tracker** – A smart, real-time solution to track and improve garbage collection across the city.

---

## **Slide 2: 🚧 The Challenge We Face**
- ❌ **Unreliable Garbage Collection** – Residents lack timely updates on pickups.
- 🗑️ **Overflowing Bins & Litter** – Poor waste management harms public health.
- 🏙️ **Limited Community Engagement** – Residents lack an easy way to report waste issues.

---

## **Slide 3: 🎯 Who Benefits?**
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Nairobi Residents** – Get live updates on garbage collection.
- 🏛️ **Local Authorities** – Improve efficiency and service delivery.
- 🌱 **Environmental Activists** – Support sustainable urban cleanup efforts.

---

## **Slide 4: 🔑 What Makes This Unique?**
✔️ **Live Collection Schedules** – Know when trash will be picked up.
✔️ **Bin Status Updates** – See which bins are full or empty.
✔️ **Easy Waste Reporting** – Instantly notify authorities of missed collections.
✔️ **Community Leaderboard** – Recognizes top contributors to cleaner streets.
✔️ **Dark Mode Toggle** – Enhances user experience with an eye-friendly interface.

---

## **Slide 5: 💡 How It Works**
🛠️ **Built With:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
📂 **Data Storage:** Local Storage for reports & leaderboard tracking
🚀 **Key Functionalities:**
  - 📝 **Submit Reports** – Report waste hotspots in seconds.
  - 🏆 **Track Community Efforts** – See who’s leading in clean-up reports.
  - 🌙 **Dark Mode** – Save your preferred viewing experience.
  - 🗑️ **Manage Reports** – Users can edit and delete reports dynamically.

---

## **Slide 6: 📢 Spreading the Word**
📱 **Social Media Campaigns** – Leverage Twitter, Facebook & WhatsApp to reach residents.
🏘️ **Community Workshops** – Educate people on proper waste management and how to use the platform.
🏅 **Partnerships with Local Authorities** – Collaborate to improve city-wide cleanup efforts.
